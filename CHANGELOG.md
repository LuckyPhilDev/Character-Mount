## [1.4.0] - 2026-06-13

### Added
- A search box at the top of the mount list filters your mounts by name as you type.

## [1.3.3] - 2026-06-07

### Improved
- Updated for the latest game patch.

## [1.3.2] - 2026-06-06

### Added
- The new mount prompt now shows a live 3D model of the mount you just unlocked, right next to the dialog.
- New setting to turn the 3D mount preview on or off.

## [1.3.1] - 2026-06-05

### Fixed
- Minimap button now appears correctly on the minimap instead of sometimes hiding behind it.
- Removed a black square that could appear when hovering the minimap button.

### Improved
- Move the minimap button with a simple click and drag.

## [1.3.0] - 2026-05-04

### Added
- When you unlock a new mount, a dialog now appears offering to add it to your list straight away.
- From the prompt, you can add the mount to just the current character or all characters at once.
- New setting to disable the new mount prompt if you prefer to manage your list manually.
